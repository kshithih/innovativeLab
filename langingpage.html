about us


"Each time a women stands up for herself, without knowing it possibly, without claiming it, she stands up for all women."

The best protection a woman can have is... courage. And if you have that courage then we are here to stand with you. Our aim is to help those woman who are facing any gender biased issues, We aim is to provide them legal support along with the stable life by educating them, teaching skills .So that they can have a successful, happy life ahead.

We aspire to provide females who identify as low-income with access to free menstrual products. Our goal is to set up Menstruation Stations in cities all over the United States to make sure no woman is left without resources!Menstruation Station makes it easy for any woman to have access to period products. To reduce financial stress, we have made access to products free of charge! So, anyone who wants to take products can simply do volunteer work. This way all members of the community benefit!
